Legal Doc Platform

After watching my family struggle to find important paperwork related to a loved one's estate, it became apparent that an organized and easy-to-access process did not exist in the process of estate planning or in settling someone's estate after they pass away.


I wanted to build a product designed to eliminate the amount of paperwork as well as the uncertainties in what to do next that exist in this process.
