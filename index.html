Hey! I'm Elizabeth. I believe designers with the most groundbreaking work are always one step ahead of what society currently wants or needs. I'm extremely fascinated with the process and psychology of designing for the future and am eager and determined to push the limits of what kinds of problems are possible to solve.


Although I was born an artist, I've always had an innate desire to problem-solve, to improve, to fix and to invent. I'm always trying to determine the most efficient way to do something. And I think my ability to intuitively design for the user stems from this natural combination of creative and structured.


Ultimately, I love to throw myself into everything. My life is always most fulfilling when I'm traveling, trail running, rock climbing, reading, learning something new, or working on something off my infinite list of creative side projects.


Enjoy exploring my designs.
